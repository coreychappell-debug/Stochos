Context: supplemental, not primary
Lottery funds represent less than 1.5% of all K–12 education funding in California. They are designed to supplement — not replace — state and local appropriations. The high prize payout rate is the primary strategy to maximize ticket sales volume, which in turn maximizes the education contribution.
