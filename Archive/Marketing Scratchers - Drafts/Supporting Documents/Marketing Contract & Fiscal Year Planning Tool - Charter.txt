# Marketing Contract & Fiscal Year Planning Tool – Updated Charter

## Purpose

A self-contained, offline-capable HTML/JavaScript/CSS application to manage post-award scratcher game contract planning. Enables Contract Managers to:

* Build and adjust annual fiscal year plans by price point.
* Compare vendor capabilities and pricing.
* Track costs, margins, and allocations in real time.
* Export reports for executives, finance, and budget teams.

## Core Goals

1. **No-Install, Portable Application**

   * Single HTML file, runs in any modern browser.
   * Optional PWA “install” for desktop-like use.

2. **Vendor Capability & Pricing Management**

   * Preloaded vendor feature database (Scientific Games, Pollard Banknote, Brightstar).
   * Cross-vendor feature mapping to generic categories.
   * Fields for base price, one-time costs, discount notes, MOQ, lead time, compatibility, exclusivity, compliance.

3. **Plan Building & Tracking**

   * Grouped by price point with sales % targets.
   * Game-by-game planning with vendor assignments and selected features.
   * Live recalculation of revenue, units, COGS, and margins.
   * Alerts for allocation over/under, missing data, or negative margins.

4. **Export & Save**

   * Save/load full plans via JSON.
   * Export CSV/XLSX summary and detailed cost reports.

## Refinements Added to Phase 1

Built in from the start to prevent re-work:

1. **Mutually Exclusive Feature Logic** – Flag or block combinations of incompatible features.
2. **Per-Year vs. Per-Game One-Time Cost Flag** – In CSV and JSON schema to allow correct cost allocation.
3. **Mid-Year Denomination Changes** – Editable denomination list without breaking existing plan totals.

## Phase 2 Enhancements

To be added after the core tool is fully functional:

1. **Structured Discount Table** – Quantity thresholds and % off with automated calculations.
2. **Lead Time Launch Date Warnings** – Planned launch date field + auto-check against lead time.
3. **Audit Trail Export/Archive** – Save year-end history separately to keep tool fast.
4. **Advanced Scheduling View** – Optional timeline visualizations for planned game launches.

## Additional Planning Data Fields

Integrated for better post-award management:

* **MOQ per Feature**
* **Lead Time / Production Time Estimates**
* **Compatible Price Points / Ticket Sizes**
* **Vendor-Specific Limitations or Exclusives**
* **Sustainability/Compliance Indicators**
* **Testing / Approval Requirements**

## Milestones

1. **Core Shell & Data Engine (1–2 weeks)**

   * Build HTML/JS/CSS structure.
   * Load master vendor feature CSV.
   * Save/load plan as JSON.
   * IndexedDB for autosave.

2. **Planning & Calculation Engine (2–3 weeks)**

   * UI for denominations & games.
   * Live calculation formulas for revenue, units, and margins.
   * Progress bars and allocation targets.

3. **Vendor Costing & Reporting (2 weeks)**

   * Right-panel vendor cost inputs linked to calculations.
   * Export CSV/XLSX (summary & detailed reports).

4. **UX Polish & Finalization (1 week)**

   * Undo/redo.
   * Audit trail display.
   * Confirmation dialogs.
   * PWA manifest and testing.
